How do i pay to use the bulk sms feature?

  • Using your MPESA-enabled phone, select “Pay Bill” from the M-PESA menu
  • Enter the Account Number USERNAME {replace with the username you use to log into the system. Use exact spelling and casing}
  • Enter the amount of credits you want to buy {ensure you add 14% VAT else it will be deducted on your amount}
  • Confirm that all the details are correct and press Ok
  • Enter your MPESA PIN. Wait for the MPESA Confirmation message.In case you face a problem eg you cannot trace your username or you have forgotten it, or you have paid but the money is not reflecting, reach us chat

How do i get a sender ID that identifies my business?

  • Login in ABSMS via the url http://{our hosted sms service on AWS}/myaccount
  • On the left menu click on Sender ID field
  • And then click on button “ADD NEW”
  • After selecting the tab, a dialog box will pop up
  • After clicking another dialog box will get opened asking for selecting a plan
Upon completion of the above steps, you will receive a notification from AB-Tech indicating success of your created sender ID, If at all the same sender ID that you have created exists, you will also receive a notification requesting for you to change, Our services are also available on chat as below

How do i get to use Google business messaging?

  • Login in ABSMS via the url http://{our hosted sms service on AWS}/myaccount
  • Open the Business Communications Developer Console and sign in with your Business Messages Google account
  • Click Account settings and then select “ADD NEW”
  • Make sure the correct partner account is selected
  • Enter your Business Messages webhook URL.(this is where we will source your business information, including your catalogue of services
  • Click Save

How do i get to use whatsapp to be able to access the services i offer?

  • Login in ABSMS via the url http://{our hosted sms service on AWS}/myaccount
  • Open the Whatsapp business menu item and sign in with your Whatsapp account
  • Click Account settings and then select “ADD NEW”
  • Make sure the correct partner account is selected
  • Enter your business menu webhook URL.(this is where we will source your whatsapp menu information, including your catalogue of services
  • Click Save
We ease the process of availing whatsapp once you are registered compared to other platforms which require you to go through the code integration process.
